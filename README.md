# 📰 AI Fake News Early Warning System

🚀 Live Demo: https://ai-fake-news-early-warning-system.onrender.com

An intelligent AI-powered platform designed to detect fake news, analyze misinformation patterns, predict viral risk, and provide explainable insights before large-scale information spread.

---

## 🚀 Project Overview

The **AI Fake News Early Warning System** is an advanced misinformation detection platform that combines Machine Learning, Explainable AI, Risk Assessment, and News Propagation Analysis.

Unlike traditional fake news classifiers, this system not only predicts whether a news article is fake or real but also explains the reasons behind the prediction and estimates the potential risk of misinformation spreading.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* Detect Fake and Real News
* Analyze News Content DNA
* Predict Misinformation Risk
* Track Information Propagation
* Provide Explainable AI Insights
* Generate Risk Reports and Visual Analytics

---

## 🧬 Core Modules

### 1. Fake News Detection Engine

Uses Machine Learning techniques to classify news content as:

* ✅ Real News
* ❌ Fake News

---

### 2. News DNA Analyzer

Analyzes news content and generates:

* Emotion Score
* Bias Score
* Evidence Score
* Clickbait Score
* Source Credibility Score

Example:

```text
Emotion Score      : 82%
Bias Score         : High
Evidence Score     : Low
Clickbait Score    : Medium
Credibility Score  : 40%
```

---

### 3. Early Warning System

Predicts the risk level before misinformation becomes viral.

Outputs:

```text
Fake Risk      : 68%
Virality Risk  : 91%
Alert Level    : High
```

---

### 4. Explainable AI Module

Provides reasons behind the prediction.

Example:

```text
Reasons:

✓ Excessive emotional language
✓ High clickbait indicators
✓ Limited supporting evidence
✓ Similar to previous misinformation patterns
```

---

### 5. Propagation Tracker

Tracks how information evolves over time.

Example:

```text
Original News
      ↓
Version 2
      ↓
Version 3
      ↓
Current Viral Version
```

---

### 6. Visual Analytics Dashboard

Interactive visualizations using Chart.js:

* DNA Analysis Graph
* Risk Assessment Dashboard
* Misinformation Indicators
* News Analysis Reports
